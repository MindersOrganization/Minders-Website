Minders

Minders is a student activity founded in 2014 by "Dr. Mohamed Bilal", Dean of the Faculty of Computers and Artificial Intelligence at Helwan University. It is present in various universities like Helwan University, Cairo University, the Egyptian Chinese University, as well as the Misr university for science and technology and it is still expanding to more universities.
